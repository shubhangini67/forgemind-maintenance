Rolling Mill Motor - Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)
Equipment: RM-002 Rolling Mill Motor
Location: Hot Rolling Mill

1. STARTUP CHECKLIST
- Verify motor current is within 45-65A range at nominal load
- Check vibration levels below 4.0 mm/s RMS
- Confirm bearing temperature below 75°C
- Inspect lubrication system oil level and flow rate

2. NORMAL OPERATING PARAMETERS
- Temperature: 60-75°C
- Vibration: 1.5-4.0 mm/s
- Pressure (cooling): 90-130 psi
- Motor current: 45-65A

3. ALARM THRESHOLDS
- Vibration > 6.0 mm/s: WARNING - schedule inspection within 24 hours
- Vibration > 8.0 mm/s: CRITICAL - stop motor and inspect bearings
- Temperature > 85°C: WARNING - check cooling system
- Temperature > 95°C: CRITICAL - emergency shutdown

4. BEARING MAINTENANCE
- Lubricate bearings every 500 operating hours with ISO VG 220 oil
- Replace bearings every 8000 hours or upon vibration exceedance
- Common fault code E-2041 indicates bearing overheating

5. TROUBLESHOOTING
Symptom: High vibration + elevated temperature
Probable cause: Bearing wear or misalignment
Action: Perform vibration analysis, inspect coupling alignment, check bearing clearance

Symptom: Motor current spike without load change
Probable cause: Winding insulation degradation or rotor bar crack
Action: Perform insulation resistance test (megger), schedule motor overhaul

6. SPARE PARTS
- Bearing 6205-2RS (Part: BRG-6205) - reorder at 5 units
- 75kW Motor Winding Kit (Part: MTR-75K) - lead time 30 days
