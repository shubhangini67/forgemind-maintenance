STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE — CCM SEGMENT ROLL BEARING ROUTE
Document: SPMW-SOP-CCM-001 | Rev 1.0 | Owner: Reliability Engineering

=== SCOPE ===
Inspection, lubrication, and replacement of segment roll bearings on SPMW-monitored CCM strands.
Applies to assets tagged equipment_type=continuous_caster in Fleet Registry.

=== EQUIPMENT ===
- CCM-STR-1 / STR-2 / STR-3 segment rolls (positions 1–12 per strand)
- Bearings: FAG 23264-K-MB (main), FAG 22222-E1 (guide rolls)

=== SPMW ALARM BANDS (mapped S1/S3) ===
Vibration RMS (S1):
  Normal: < 4.5 mm/s
  Investigate: 4.5 – 5.5 mm/s — increase sampling to 15 min
  Hold cast: > 9.0 mm/s — stop strand after safe tail-out

Bearing temperature (S3):
  Normal: 42 – 72°C
  Investigate: 72 – 82°C
  Hold cast: > 88°C sustained 10 min

=== LUBRICATION (SPMW-F412 prevention) ===
Grease: Klüberplex BEM 41-132
1. Lock out strand segment per LOTO-CCM-12
2. Clean nipple; apply 25 g per point at ≤ 3 bar
3. Rotate roll 3 revolutions by hand
4. Log tonnage counter + technician ID in digital logbook

=== REPLACEMENT TRIGGER ===
Replace bearing when ANY:
- Hold-cast band exceeded twice in 7 days
- SPMW RUL < vendor lead time for SKF/FAG equivalent
- Visual spalling or grease contamination (dark emulsion)

=== POST-WORK VERIFICATION ===
1. Alignment check ±0.25 mm vs partner roll
2. Baseline S1 capture within 2 h of restart
3. Close work order with fault tag cleared in CMMS
