Failure Analysis Report - Rolling Mill Motor Bearing Failure
Report ID: FAR-2025-0142
Equipment: RM-002 Rolling Mill Motor
Date: 2025-03-15

INCIDENT SUMMARY
The rolling mill motor experienced unplanned shutdown due to bearing failure. Fault code E-2041 was triggered when vibration exceeded 8.2 mm/s and bearing temperature reached 102°C.

SYMPTOMS OBSERVED
- Vibration increased from 3.1 to 8.2 mm/s over 48 hours
- Temperature rose from 68°C to 102°C
- Motor current increased by 12% without load change
- Audible grinding noise from drive end bearing

ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS
Primary cause: Insufficient lubrication due to blocked oil passage in lubrication pump filter.
Contributing factors:
1. Lubrication interval exceeded by 120 operating hours
2. Vibration trending was not flagged early enough
3. Spare bearing stock was below reorder level (1 unit remaining)

CORRECTIVE ACTIONS TAKEN
1. Replaced both drive-end and non-drive-end bearings (6205-2RS)
2. Cleaned and replaced lubrication filter
3. Updated lubrication schedule in CMMS
4. Calibrated vibration sensors

RECOMMENDATIONS
- Implement automated vibration threshold alerts at 6.0 mm/s
- Maintain minimum 5 bearing units in inventory
- Perform monthly lubrication system inspection
- Enable predictive maintenance monitoring on RM-002

DOWNTIME IMPACT
Total downtime: 12 hours
Production loss: 480 tons hot rolled coil
Estimated cost: $180,000
