Blast Furnace Blower - Equipment Manual
Equipment: BF-001 Blast Furnace Blower
Manufacturer: Tata Steel OEM

OVERVIEW
The blast furnace blower provides combustion air to the blast furnace. Failure results in immediate production stoppage with estimated downtime cost of $125,000/hour.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
- Rated power: 2500 kW
- Operating temperature range: 55-90°C
- Normal vibration: 2.0-5.0 mm/s
- Design pressure: 100-140 psi

PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE
- Daily: Visual inspection, temperature and vibration check
- Weekly: Belt tension verification, filter inspection
- Monthly: Bearing lubrication, alignment check
- Quarterly: Full vibration spectrum analysis

COMMON FAILURE MODES
1. Impeller erosion - causes vibration increase and pressure drop
2. Bearing failure - caused by contamination or insufficient lubrication
3. Drive belt wear - causes speed fluctuation and current oscillation

RECOMMENDED ACTIONS FOR DEGRADATION
When health indicator drops below 70:
- Increase monitoring frequency to every 4 hours
- Schedule impeller inspection within 72 hours
- Verify spare belt availability (Part: BLT-A42)

SAFETY
Always lockout/tagout before maintenance. Allow 30-minute cooldown before bearing inspection.
